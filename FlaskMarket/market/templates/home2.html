Handling and Best Practices

Guidelines and best practices for handling livestock promote their well-being. The well-being of livestock is paramount, encompassing their physical and mental health. Proper handling plays a crucial role in ensuring this well-being, reducing stress, preventing injuries, and promoting productivity. Here are some essential guidelines and best practicesThese include: Regular Observation and Monitoring: Daily Observation: Observe animals daily for signs of illness, injury, or stress. Early detection of problems is crucial for effective treatment. Record Keeping: Maintain accurate records of animal health, behavior, and productivity.
